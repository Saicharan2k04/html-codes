mythical creatures

dragon
A dragon is a large magical legendary creature that appears in the folklore of multiple cultures worldwide. Beliefs about dragons vary considerably through regions, but dragons in Western cultures since the High Middle Ages have often been depicted as winged, horned, and capable of breathing fire.
werewolf
Werewolves have been depicted in different ways throughout history. In ancient Arcadian legends, human beings transform fully into, and associate with, wolves. In other traditions, particularly Hollywood horror films, werewolves are humanoid, often featuring a canine head, claws, and a fur-covered human body.
phoenix
The phoenix bird is a mythical creature that is associated with the sun and symbolizes themes of rebirth, immortality, and transformation. It is depicted as a bird with colorful plumage and is associated with the cyclical nature of life and the rejuvenating power of the sun's energy
